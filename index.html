About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Tsukuba and a member of the Semiconductor Nanostructure Group at the National Institute for Materials Science (NIMS). My research focuses on GeSn epitaxy by molecular beam epitaxy (MBE) and group-IV photonic devices.

Research Interests

  • GeSn Epitaxy
  • Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE)
  • Group-IV Photonics
  • Infrared Optoelectronic Devices
  • Semiconductor micro/nano processing
